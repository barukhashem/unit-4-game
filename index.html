Dino Collector

At the start of each game, a random number is shown.

There are four dinosaurs below. Clicking on a dinosaur adds a certain number of points to your score.

The value of each dinosaur is unknown until you click on it.

You WIN if your score MATCHES the random number.

You LOSE if your score EXCEEDS the random number.

For each new game, the value of each dinosaur randomnly changes.
