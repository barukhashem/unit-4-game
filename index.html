Dino-Score!


For each game, four dinosaurs and a random number are shown below.

Clicking a dinosaur adds a certain number of points to your Dino-Score.

However, the value of each dinosaur is unknown until you click it.

You WIN if your score EQUALS the random number.

You LOSE if your score EXCEEDS the random number.

For each new game, the value of each dinosaur randomnly changes.
