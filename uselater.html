Crystal Collector

Up for another challenge, you coding superstar, you?

At the start of each game, a random number is displayed.

There are four crystals below. Clicking on a crystal adds a certain number of points to your score.

You WIN if your score MATCHES the random number.

You LOSE if your score EXCEEDS the random number.

The numerical value of each crystal is unknown until you click on it.

For each new game, the numerical value of each crystal randomnly changes.
